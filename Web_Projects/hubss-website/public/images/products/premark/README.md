## Shot guidance

- Show a variety of symbols on an asphalt surface: arrows, bike symbols, SLOW legend, etc.
- Overhead shot works best — shows the full symbol shape clearly
- Crisp white on dark asphalt is the visual goal
- A "symbols ready to install" flat-lay can work well as a secondary shot
