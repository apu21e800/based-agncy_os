## Shot guidance

- Lead with the most striking custom design in HUB's portfolio — Pride crosswalk, Indigenous art, or community mural
- Aerial or elevated shot captures the full artwork; ground-level shows vibrancy
- Include people if possible (with permission) — it communicates scale and community connection
- Colour accuracy matters here: the product's key promise is "Pantone-matched colour"
