## Shot guidance

- Vivid bike lane or pedestrian corridor — show the colour saturation
- Green, red, or blue bike lane on grey asphalt reads instantly as "StreetBond"
- Wide shot works best to communicate the scale of coverage
- A sun-lit daytime shot shows colour vibrancy; avoids the "faded" concern buyers have
