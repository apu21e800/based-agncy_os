## Shot guidance

- Clean, freshly-sealed and re-marked parking lot — crisp white lines on dark DuraShield surface
- Slightly elevated angle shows the stall grid layout and accessible parking symbols
- Commercial or municipal lot (not residential) reinforces the B2B positioning
- Avoid shots with damaged, cracked, or faded surfaces — the product message is "restoration"
