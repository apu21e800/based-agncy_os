| Filename        | Key in images.ts   | Subject                                    |
|-----------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| `team.jpg`      | `about_team`       | Full team photo or crew on a job site      |
| `story.jpg`     | `about_story`      | Founders, early project, or Canadian city aerial |

## Shot guidance

- `team.jpg`: Candid crew shot on site is more credible than a staged office photo
- `story.jpg`: Canadian city infrastructure or an early landmark project
- Offices: Clean exterior daylight, signage visible if possible

## Naming convention

Use exact filenames above — they match the swap instructions in `lib/images.ts` comments.
