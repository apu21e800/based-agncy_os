# /public/images/hero

Site-wide hero background used on the homepage.

Referenced in `lib/images.ts` → `SITE_IMAGES.hero_bg`.

## Files

| Filename       | Used in            | Subject                                          |
|----------------|--------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| `hero-bg.jpg`  | Homepage hero      | Aerial or wide shot of a colourful Canadian crosswalk or decorated streetscape |

## Dimensions

| Slot      | Width  | Height | Notes                        |
|-----------|--------|--------|------------------------------|
| `hero-bg` | 1600px | 900px  | 16:9 — fill-cover, so keep subject centred |

## Shot guidance

- Aerial or elevated angle works best (shows the full street surface)
- Strong colour contrast — orange, red, or teal markings on dark asphalt
- Avoid portraits or close-up faces (licensing / privacy)
- Examples: decorative crosswalk, Vision Zero corridor, StreetPrint plaza

## Naming convention

`hero-bg.jpg` — exact filename expected by `lib/images.ts`.
